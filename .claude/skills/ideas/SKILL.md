# Ideas — Vault-Grounded Idea Report

Scan King's vault for emerging patterns and generate a concrete idea report.

## Process

Read in parallel:
- All `~/Documents/Obsidian/personalOS/Knowledge/` notes — what he's thinking about deeply
- `~/Documents/Obsidian/personalOS/Inbox.md` and `~/Documents/Obsidian/personalOS/Someday Maybe.md` — latent interests
- Last 14 days of `~/Documents/Obsidian/personalOS/Daily Notes/` — recent energy and recurring themes
- `~/Documents/Obsidian/personalOS/Projects/` (active) — what he's already building
- `~/Documents/Obsidian/personalOS/Areas/` — ongoing responsibilities that might spawn ideas
- `~/Documents/Obsidian/personalOS/Reviews/` (most recent 2) — what he said he wanted to explore

Look for:
- Topics that appear in multiple folders (signal = high interest)
- Questions he keeps asking without answering
- Half-formed thoughts in daily notes that recur
- Gaps between what he knows and what he's building
- People or communities mentioned repeatedly

## Output Format

---

**Idea Report — [date]**

**Tools / projects to build**
- [idea] — *why: [pattern from vault]*
- [idea] — *why: [pattern from vault]*

**People to reach out to**
- [person or type of person] — *why: [what King is working on that connects]*

**Topics to investigate**
- [topic] — *why: [keeps appearing in X, Y, Z notes without resolution]*

**Things to write**
- [piece or note] — *why: [King has the raw material in these notes]*

**The strongest signal**
[The single most compelling idea from the above, with fuller reasoning]

---

## Rules

- Every idea must be grounded in specific vault evidence — no generic suggestions
- "Why" is mandatory for every idea — pattern-backed, not intuition-backed
- Prioritize ideas at the intersection of multiple domains King cares about
- The strongest signal should be something King probably hasn't consciously noticed
